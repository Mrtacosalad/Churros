Why Churros Are Awesome

Churros are little fried sticks of happiness! Crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and covered in cinnamon sugar, they’re the perfect handheld snack. Whether you’re enjoying them at a festival, a sports game, or just as a homemade treat, churros never disappoint.

The Best Churros I've Ever Had

While all churros are great, some are unforgettable:

  • Homemade Churros: My mother’s churros will always be my favorite. There’s just something extra special about a homemade batch!
  • California Vendor: Dulce de leche-stuffed churros from a California street vendor—pure bliss in every bite.
  • Climate Pledge Arena: A memorable churro at a Kraken’s game that added a sweet kick to an exciting night.

Costco’s Churro Blunder

Costco may be known for its epic snacks, but removing churros from the menu was a mistake. Even though they weren’t the best churros, they had a special place in every Costco-goer’s heart. Replacing them with a chocolate chip cookie? Sacrilege!

The Churro Experience

What makes churros truly special is their versatility. They can be enjoyed plain or filled with chocolate, caramel, or even fruit. And let’s not forget how perfectly they pair with a warm cup of coffee or hot chocolate. Churros are a snack that brings people together, one sugary bite at a time.
